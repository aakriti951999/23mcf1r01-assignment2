KHUSH MAHAL

Khush Mahal, also known as Shitab Khan’s Palace, is a historical structure located within the Warangal Fort complex. Constructed during the Qutb Shahi dynasty, it showcases a fusion of Persian and Indian architectural styles. Although partially in ruins, Khush Mahal still bears witness to its former grandeur and provides visitors with a glimpse into the region’s diverse historical influences.

This is the Khush Mahal aka Sitab Khan Mahal. It served as the audience hall for the erstwhile governor of #Warangal after whom it is named. It is places like these outside #Hyderabad that most tourists to the state of #Telengana in #India miss out on.
